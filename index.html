Everything you need to bake beautiful creations, delivered straight to your door


Premium ingredients

Boxes are filled with hand-selected ingredients from our trusted suppliers. We provide the exact quantities required for your recipes to avoid waste and use completely sustainable packaging.

Bespoke equipment

Each box contains a tool, accessory or piece of equipment to help you make your best creations. Already have some equipment at home? Just fill in the checklist in your account to let us know, and we'll make sure we don't send you something you already own.

Unique recipes

Each box contains three original, creative recipes written by our team of expert bakers. Our recipe library covers every taste and dietary requirement - sweet, savoury, vegan, gluten free and many, many more. Fill in your personal taste profile and we'll send recipes that match your preferences.
